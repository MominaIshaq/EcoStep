Eco-Friendly Tips

Simple changes that make a big difference for our planet

Use Reusable Bottles

Switch to reusable water bottles and reduce plastic waste by up to 1,460 bottles per year.

Saves 2 trees annually

Use Public Transport

Take the bus, train, or bike instead of driving. Public transport reduces CO2 emissions significantly.

Saves 5 trees annually

Save Electricity

Switch to LED bulbs, unplug devices when not in use, and turn off lights when leaving rooms.

Saves 3 trees annually

Recycle & Reuse

Properly sort your waste, recycle materials, and find creative ways to reuse items before throwing them away.

Saves 4 trees annually

Reduce Food Waste

Plan your meals, store food properly, and compost organic waste to minimize your food footprint.

Saves 6 trees annually

Bring Your Own Bags

Use reusable shopping bags instead of plastic bags. One reusable bag can replace 1000+ plastic bags.

Saves 1 tree annually
